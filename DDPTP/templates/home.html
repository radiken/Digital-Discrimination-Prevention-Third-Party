A Privacy-Aware Discrimination Prevention Workflow

The workflow is proposed to prevent discrimination and protect privacy.
It is a carefully designed and tested process for organizations and individuals to follow,
in the context that organizations use algorithms to classify individuals.

Prevents Discrimination

Prevent discrimination from algorithms easily with the help of the third-party application. Value individuals' right.

Protects Privacy

Protect individuals' sensitive information from being accessed by decision-making algorithms. Eliminate discrimination from the root.

Preserves Business Insight

Allows businesses to access sensitive information statistically. Be considerate to businesses.
